About University of South Dundee

Founded in 1965, we are committed to excellence in education, research, and community engagement

Our History

The University of South Dundee was established in 1965 with a vision to provide accessible higher education to the region. Over the decades, we have grown into a comprehensive institution with a reputation for academic excellence and innovative research.

Our campus has expanded from a single building to a 50-acre site with state-of-the-art facilities, serving over 15,000 students from around the world.

Mission & Values

Our Mission: To transform lives through education, research, and innovation that addresses global challenges.

Our Values:

  • Excellence in teaching and research
  • Diversity and inclusion
  • Community engagement
  • Sustainability and ethical practice
  • Student-centered approach

Accreditations & Rankings

The University of South Dundee is proud of our recognition and achievements:

  • Ranked in the top 50 UK universities (The Guardian 2023)
  • Gold rating in Teaching Excellence Framework
  • Accredited by the Quality Assurance Agency for Higher Education
  • Recognized for outstanding research in Environmental Sciences
  • International accreditation for Business and Engineering programs

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for this program, you must meet the following requirements:

English Language Requirements

International students must provide proof of English proficiency:

  • IELTS: 6.0 overall with no less than 5.5 in any component
  • TOEFL iBT: 80 overall with minimum 18 in each component
  • Pearson PTE: 56 overall with 51 in each component

Tuition Fees & Financial Information

The following fees apply to international students for the 2023/24 academic year:

Annual Tuition Fee: €0
Initial Deposit: €4,000
Remaining Balance: €0

Payment Options

  • Full payment at the beginning of the academic year (5% discount available)
  • Two installment plan (50% at registration, 50% by January 31st)
  • Monthly payment plan (additional administration fee applies)

Scholarships & Funding

We offer various scholarships to support our students. Based on your profile, you may be eligible for:

International Excellence Scholarship

Up to €0 per year for high-achieving international students.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Academic excellence (minimum 75% in previous studies)
  • Strong personal statement
  • Evidence of extracurricular achievements

Additional Funding Options

  • Country-specific scholarships
  • Early application discount (€500 if applied before March 31st)
  • Sibling discount (10% for second family member)
  • Sports and arts scholarships
